Welcome to RSE South!

RSE South is a new regional research software engineering (RSE) network.

Founded in early 2022, RSE South aims to bring RSEs and RSE groups in the South of the UK together to network, collaborate and to share RSE skills and resources.

The network aims to reach institutions located in the South of the UK, focusing on groups outside of the existing London RSE network.


The network is open to and welcomes RSEs or RSE Groups and any new members passionate about research software from the UK South.

If you would like to join RSE South and our associated mailing list, please get in touch! Contact: Maria Broadbridge, m.b.broadbridge@reading.ac.uk

We also have an rse-south slack channel in the ukrse slack group.


RSE South Webinar series

We run a bi-monthly series of webinars on RSE topics, advertised though our mailing list and slack channel.

You can find past instalments of our webinar series on our youtube channel @rsesouthuk.


RSE SOUTH WORKSHOP IN PREPARATION!

We are pleased to announce that our first in-person RSE South Workshop is in the planning stage.

The workshop will run under the theme of "Sustainability and Reproducibility of Research Software".

It is scheduled for Tuesday, May 16th 2023 and will be held at the University of Reading.
